About

​Surangan is purely an cult​ural organization . This organization is active for not less than 6 decades, keeping social an​d moral value high with its remarkable shows. Over 5000 and above national and international performances/shows given by this organization. One can see Nationalism is key to its performances. Surangan is always keen to serve the nation through a healthy artistic creations. It is notable t​hat during the 1962 Congress Adhiveshan the organization performed a dance drama “Ye Bharat ke gaun” and again during the Indo China war Surangan performed “Ee Hammar Himalay” which was highly appreciated by the then Prime Minister Pandit Jawahar Lal Nehru. Numerous notable performances given by Surangan, few names are mention below -- “ Mehnat Ki Jeet “, “ Tilottama”, “ Hiran Hirni”, “Shanti Ki Chah Buddh Ki Rah” , “ Brahmasa “ ,” Jhijhia”, “ Domkach”, ” Jat Jatin”, “Kamla Puja” “ Chitrangada”, “ Raj Nartaki Kosha”, “Chandanwala”, “Bihar Darpan” etc. Surangan Journey – Surangan was established on 31 st December 1959. The first performance of Surangan was staged on the occasion of silver jubilee celebration of L S College , Muzaffarpur in the esteem presence of the then President Dr. Rajendra Prasad. Not looking behind from its starting , Surangan always maintained quality and performed throughout the country and abroad keeping its standard and reputation..
